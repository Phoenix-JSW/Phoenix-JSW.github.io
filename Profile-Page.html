My Story

I have always had a passion for IT, which lead me down the road to becoming a Microsoft and Novell Systems Engineer and Mail Administrator after I left the British Royal Navy. I worked in 3rd line intel server teams for British Telecom, Computer Science Corporation, JPMorgan, Chase Manhattan Bank and HSBC before Migrating to Australia where I took on a Team Leader & Project Management Role at Promina Insurance in Sydney.

After taking time out to have a family I took a role with Bunnings, completed my AIM Business Management Diploma and moved into Retail Management. I have managed a few different departments within several different stores, managing up to 40 team members at any one time. After becoming a single mum, I took a small step back to spend some quality time with my kids. I took on the role of Safety, Care & Recoveries Officer, Store Facilities & preventitive maintenance administration, with a little training thrown in for good measure. I have always taken pride in ensuring any issues are dealt with in a Smooth and Efficient manner. Having strong interpersonal skills, I have always been able to work autonomously.
